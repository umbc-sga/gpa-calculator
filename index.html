GPA Coach is a tool that tells you, given how many credits you plan on taking, what GPA you will have to get whilst taking that number of credits in order to raise your GPA to a certain target GPA.

If the GPA you need to get in order to reach your target GPA is below 0 or above a 4 then it is impossible to reach your target GPA in that amount of credits, which in that case means you either need to raise the number of credits, or lower your target.
